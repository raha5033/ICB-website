ICB Masjid Potluck

Community Potluck
First Friday of Every Month
Join Us for a Blessed Evening of Food & Fellowship

Our community masjid welcomes everyone—Muslims and non-Muslims—to our monthly **Potluck Night**. Bring a dish, snack, or anything you can afford and share in the blessings of good food and great company.

The evening begins with a **short lecture after Maghrib**, followed by **dinner after Isha**. The masjid will also provide food and snacks to supplement the potluck.

Why Join Us?
  • Strengthen community bonds
  • Experience the joy of sharing
  • Engage in beneficial discussions
  • Enjoy delicious meals with good company
"The best of people are those who bring the most benefit to others." – Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

- Imam Ahmed, Community Leader
